Intro

I am a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Systems Engineering (B.S.) and Robotics (M.S., considering switch to Data Science). My current interests are in analytics, statistics, software engineering, education, teaching, writing, and entrepreneurship.

I grew up in San Francisco where I went to Lowell High School. At Penn, I found interests in a wide array of subjects from Positive Psychology to Ancient Greek History. I discovered a passion for engineering after taking Dynamical Systems (with Penn's best math professor, Robert Ghrist). I have always loved building things and hope to join a team where I can contribute to something meaningful.

I am flexible and am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow.
