Intro

My name is Sam Ryan. I am a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Systems Science & Engineering. I have always loved building things. My current interests are in education, teaching, writing, analytics, data science, software engineering and entrepreneurship.

I grew up in San Francisco where I went to Lowell High School. In college, I have found interests in a wide array of subjects from Positive Psychology to Ancient Greek History. I found a passion for engineering after taking Dynamical Systems (with Penn's best math professor Robert Ghrist) and since then have built a strong toolkit of programming and modeling techniques to solve a wide variety of problems.

I am flexible and am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow.
